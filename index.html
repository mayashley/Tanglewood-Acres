About Our Farm

TangleWood Acres has been proudly owned and opperated by the Hawkins family since 1941. We pride ourselves in the ability to offer fun and engaging family friendly adventures. TangleWood boasts 124 acres of farm land. We offer the oppertunity to spend the weekend at one of our wooded campsites, enjoy a full day of horseback riding on our expansive trails, private riding lessons, and more.
